About This Tool

MACSD Internal Tool is a web-based management system for SDLFF and DGC interns.

Film Database — manage SDLFF films and Eventive imports

CRM Directory — people and organizations with tags, priority, and filtering

Outreach Projects — manage workspaces, groups, drafts, and outreach activity

Inventory — warehouse item pickup & return tracking

Daily Workflow
STEP 1
Check CRM Directory and identify target people or organizations.
STEP 2
Use Outreach Workspace to build groups and draft messages.
STEP 3
Open Canva when you need flyers, social graphics, or campaign visuals.
